Our Story

A timeline of how we got to this point.

  • 2012

    Frosh Start

    We met during frosh week at Queen's University after being randomly entered into the same frosh group.

  • 2013

    J is for Love

    Apparently it wasn't quite love at first sight, as it took us a year or so to start dating. After an on- and off-again start, it took until December for Renee to ask "so...what are we?"

  • 2016

    East and West

    Graduation from Queen's brought an uncertain future: Renee was off to Montreal while Taylor was off to Seattle. Long distance it was. With limited funds, we'd fly back and forth every ten weeks or so. Long distance was tough, but we both agree now that it made us stronger as a couple and helped us grow individually outside of the relationship too.

  • 2017

    West and West

    Long distance (fortunately) didn't last too long. Renee moved across the country to start a new job in Vancouver. Instead of a 5-hour flight, now we were just 3-hour car ride apart. We went back and forth 2-3 times a month, much better but still room for improvement.

  • 2020

    Pandemic? What Pandemic?

    COVID-19 let Taylor escape to Vancouver for nearly the whole year -- and this was going to be the first time we had actually lived together. Taylor proposed on the last day of our first quarantine. We managed to skip the "living together" step and go right to being engaged. How's that for efficiency.
